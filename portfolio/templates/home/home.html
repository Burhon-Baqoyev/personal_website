Hey! My name’s Anna. I am a graphic designer and illustrator based in Denver, Colorado. I am passionate about design and illustration and I want to help you make your next project be beautiful.

I graduated from Iowa State University in Ames, Iowa with a B.F.A in graphic design. Since then I have worked as a freelancer multiple times, worked as a designer for HVAC interface systems, worked retail at Jo-ann fabrics, made many costumes (for myself, thank you very much, and the number continues growing), and been a professional superhero. I’m currently working as a podcast editor for College Info Geek while growing my own art business and going to fan conventions to sell my art.

When I’m not doing art you can find me customizing dolls, sewing costumes, playing video games, watching anime, writing, reading, or hanging out with my cat.
